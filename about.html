About Remote Sensing Application Centre

The Remote Sensing Application Centre (RSAC), under the Department of Science & Technology, plays a vital role in using geospatial technology to support governance, planning, and resource management.

RSAC specializes in Remote Sensing, GIS, and satellite data analytics to provide decision support tools for sectors such as agriculture, water resources, forestry, urban development, and disaster management.

Our Vision

To empower government and institutions with accurate and timely geospatial intelligence for effective planning and sustainable development.

Our Mission

  • To serve as a Centre of Excellence in remote sensing and GIS technologies.
  • To develop innovative applications using satellite data and AI/ML tools.
  • To support e-governance through spatial data infrastructure.
  • To conduct research and training in geospatial domains.

Key Functions

  • Mapping and monitoring natural resources.
  • Supporting urban and rural planning.
  • Generating thematic layers using satellite imagery.
  • Developing decision support systems using GIS.
  • Capacity building and technical training.
